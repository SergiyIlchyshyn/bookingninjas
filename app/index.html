Core Components of Booking Ninjas Property Management System

Reservation System

Cloud-Based & Mobile Friendly

  • Know the real-time status of rooms & reservations made
  • Keep everyone from staff to guests informed

Why a reservation system within the PMS?

The standard the Central Reservations System (CRS) sits above the property management level to manage and distribute Availability, Rates, and Inventory (ARI) to guests via various channels.

The Property Management System (PMS), meanwhile, manages all of the on-premises management needs, such as housekeeping, POS, guest check-in, and check-out, among others.

But Booking Ninjas combines the features of both into one easy, simple application, making access and use simple, efficient, and quick. This saves you time, allowing you to focus on giving your guests a better experience

Rooms Availability Status Grid

Availability Hour Grid (AHG) to Improve Resource Planning

Gain complete oversight of all rooms and reservations with an easy drag and drop interface with our Availability Hour Grid (AHG). Plan resource allocation well in advance and keep a look out for current and future availabilities.

Our Reservation Management “Command Center” features:

  • A drag and drop interface allowing staff to change, re-assign or move rooms
  • Manage property cleaning statuses
  • Reservation management - search, make and look up reservations with simple and advanced filters

Room Rate Controller (RRC)

Our AI-Based Yield Management Pricing System helps with:

Attempting to coordinate and manage pricing dynamism with legacy PMSes can be slow, unintuitive, and take up a lot of time due to manual work. But that’s no longer an issue with Booking Ninjas’ integrated, AI-powered management pricing system.

Experience absolute control over present and future room rates.

Our AI algorithm will help you easily identify the best revenue sources with intelligent rule-based pricing strategies – so you can be assured that you’re not missing out on revenue opportunities.

Importantly, it integrates fully and seamlessly with external booking sites such as Agoda or Booking.com – say goodbye to multiple logins and manual labor!

You can also choose to manually or automatically set room rates, and even send mass updates to all your booking channels and engines.

Key technical features:

  • Manage rate restrictions, daily pricing changes, monthly, hourly and minimum pricing.
  • Is fully connected to external booking sites such as Booking.com and Agoda
  • Allows you to set up pricing rules based on occupancy levels and other rule-based pricing strategies
  • Pricing is fully connected to Booking Engine

Mobile App

For Owners, Staff and Guests


Our mobile app is a one-stop destination that allows property managers or owners to conveniently and efficiently control what happens in the backend.


Additionally, it features multiple modes with appropriate controls and functions for your staff, housekeeping, travel agents, as well as guests.


Here’s a brief breakdown of what you’ll get:


For Owners


Real-time information is just a click away. Immediately see information about the management company you’ve hired, on top of occupancy rates, guest profiles, as well as other key property performance information.

You can also book or reserve dates for your own personal use, approve work order expenses, or other property improvement budgets that the management company is tasked to oversee.


For Staff


Staff will find the system that’s fully synced with the central PMS system easy to navigate and helps them be more efficient with their work.


Features:


  • Time entry system
  • Manage cleaning schedules
  • See property management task lists
  • Self-management of their own task list
  • Notification for rooms requiring maintenance and cleaning (such as from requests)

For Guests


Residents will find the platform easy and simple to use, with these features:


  • Bill payments

  • Ordering
